About AcadTrack

Built for Filipino students,
free and private

AcadTrack helps you monitor your college progress — subjects, grades, prerequisites, and graduation timeline — all without needing an account.

What AcadTrack does

📊 GWA computation

Supports 1.0-highest, 5.0-highest, and Benilde 4.0-highest grading systems. Latin honors update live.

🔗 Prerequisite tracking

Link subjects together. Unmet prerequisites are flagged automatically.

📈 Progress tracking

See your degree completion percentage by year and semester, including credited subjects.

🗓 Graduation estimate

Supports semestral, trimestral, and quadrimestral calendars.

🔮 What-if Simulator New

Assign hypothetical grades to remaining subjects and see your projected GWA in real time.

⚠ Irregular detection New

Automatically flags your account as Irregular if ongoing subjects span more than one year level.

★ Credited subjects New

Mark transferred or cross-enrolled subjects as Credited — counts toward units, excluded from GWA.

🏫 50 schools New

Searchable dropdown of 50 Philippine universities with auto-detected grading and calendar type.

📚 Curriculum templates

Load a pre-built curriculum to instantly populate subjects. Adamson BSBA Marketing (2022) is available now.

🔒 No account needed

Everything is saved in your browser's local storage. Nothing is ever uploaded anywhere.

Your data & sync

💾

Where is my data saved?

AcadTrack saves everything in your browser's localStorage — it stays on your device and is never uploaded anywhere.

📵

Does it sync between my phone and PC?

No — not automatically. localStorage is device and browser-specific. Use Export/Import to move data between devices. Always export before clearing browser data.

⚡ How to sync between devices

Use Export and Import in Settings → Data & backup.

1
Open Settings ⚙ on the Dashboard → Export to file. A .json file downloads.
2
Transfer it to your other device — via Google Drive, email, Messenger, or USB.
3
On the new device, open Settings ⚙ → Import from file. Select the .json file.
4
Your tracker is synced. Repeat whenever you make major updates.

Tip: Pick one primary device for updating grades, then Export/Import to sync to your phone or tablet.

About the project

AcadTrack was built to help Filipino college students stay on top of their academic progress without messy spreadsheets or hard-to-navigate school portals.

It works for any Philippine university — UP, Ateneo, La Salle, Adamson, UST, Benilde, and any other school — with support for all major grading systems and calendar types used across the country.

Your privacy matters. AcadTrack does not track usage, collect analytics, show ads, or send your data anywhere. Everything lives in your browser.
