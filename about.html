About AcadTrack

Built for Filipino students,
free and private

AcadTrack helps you monitor your college progress — subjects, grades, prerequisites, and graduation timeline — all without needing an account.

What AcadTrack does

📊 GWA computation

Supports the Philippine 1.0-highest and 5.0-highest systems. GWA updates live as you log grades, with honor labels (Cum laude, etc.).

🔗 Prerequisite tracking

Link subjects together. Subjects with unmet prerequisites are flagged automatically so you know what to take first.

📈 Progress tracking

See your degree completion percentage based on earned units vs. total curriculum units — broken down by year and semester.

🗓 Graduation estimate

Based on remaining semester count and the Philippine academic calendar (5 months per sem), AcadTrack estimates your finish date.

📚 Curriculum templates

Load a pre-built curriculum to instantly populate all your subjects. Adamson BSBA Marketing Management (2022) is available now, with more coming.

🔒 No account needed

Everything is stored in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your academic data is yours alone.

Your data & sync

💾

Where is my data saved?

AcadTrack saves your data in your browser's localStorage — a built-in storage area in every modern browser. It stays on your device and is never uploaded anywhere.

📵

Does it sync between my phone and PC?

No — not automatically. localStorage is specific to the browser and device you're using. If you add subjects on your phone, those changes will not appear on your PC's browser, and vice versa.

This also means: if you clear your browser data or use a different browser on the same device, your tracker data may be lost.

⚡ How to use AcadTrack across multiple devices

Use the Export and Import feature (Settings → Data & backup) to move your data between devices manually.

1
On your current device: open Settings ⚙ on the dashboard and click Export to file. A .json file will download.
2
Transfer the file to your other device — via Google Drive, email, Messenger, USB, or any method you prefer.
3
On the new device: go to acadtrack in your browser, open Settings ⚙, and click Import from file. Select the .json file.
4
Your tracker is now synced. Repeat whenever you make major updates on one device.

Tip: Pick one primary device (e.g. your PC) for updating grades, and use Export/Import whenever you want to view it on your phone. Automatic cloud sync may be added in a future version.

About the project

AcadTrack was built to help Filipino college students stay on top of their academic progress without needing to maintain messy spreadsheets or rely on school portals that can be hard to navigate.

It's designed to work for any Philippine university — whether you're in UP, Ateneo, La Salle, Adamson, UST, or any other school — as long as you can enter your curriculum. No account, no subscription, no data collection.

Your privacy matters. AcadTrack does not track your usage, collect analytics, show ads, or send your data anywhere. Everything lives in your browser.
