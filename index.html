What AcadTrack does
📊

GWA calculator

Supports 1.0-highest, 5.0-highest, and Benilde (4.0-highest) grading systems. GWA updates automatically as you log grades.

🔗

Prerequisite tracking

Link subjects to their prerequisites. Get warned when a prereq hasn't been passed yet before you enroll.

🗓

Graduation estimate

Supports semestral, trimestral, and quadrimestral calendars. AcadTrack estimates when you'll finish your degree.

📤

Export & import

Back up your data as a JSON file and restore it on any device. Useful for switching between phone and PC.
