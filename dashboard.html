💾 Weekly backup reminder
⚠Browser data can be cleared at any time. Clearing your browser cache, cookies, or site data will permanently delete your entire AcadTrack history — all subjects, grades, schedule, and journal entries.
📱Uninstalling or switching apps deletes local data. If you uninstall your browser, switch to a new phone, or do a factory reset, your data is gone with it.
💻Your phone and PC don't sync automatically. Data saved on your phone is separate from your PC. Export from one device and import on the other to stay in sync.
🔒No cloud backup exists. AcadTrack is privacy-first — your data never leaves your device. This means you are the only backup. One export file keeps years of academic progress safe.
How to recover: If you lose your data, simply import your latest export file — Settings > Data & backup > Import. All subjects, grades, and settings restore instantly.
